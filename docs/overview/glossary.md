# Glossary

## District
A school district that owns assets and manages campuses, users, and policies.

## Campus
A school site within a district (often synonymous with a school).

## Building
A physical building on a campus.

## Room
A physical room within a building.

## Asset
A trackable item (device, peripheral, equipment) owned by a district.

## Asset Tag
A district-issued identifier for an asset, often printed on a label.

## Assignment
The act of checking an asset out to a person for a period of time.

## Chain-of-Custody
The historical record of who held an asset and when it changed hands.

## Audit (Inventory Audit)
A scan-based reconciliation of expected assets vs found assets.

## Incident
A record of damage, loss, or theft related to an asset.

## Portal
A role-based UI view (parent, student, staff) within InventoryK12.
