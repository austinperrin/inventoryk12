# Texas Requirements

List Texas-specific legal and policy requirements here. Keep each requirement
short, plain-language, and reference the source authority.

## Draft Requirements (Needs Legal Validation)

- TX-REQ-001: Student data privacy obligations apply to student, staff, and parent/guardian PII. Sources: [TEC § 38.309](https://texas.public.law/statutes/tex._educ._code_section_38.309), [Gov't Code § 552.114](https://texas.public.law/statutes/tex._gov%27t_code_section_552.114)
- TX-REQ-002: Districts require role-based access controls with least privilege. Sources: [TEC § 38.309](https://texas.public.law/statutes/tex._educ._code_section_38.309) (privacy safeguards), [TSDS privacy guidance](https://www.texasstudentdatasystem.org/TSDS/About/Security/Privacy)
- TX-REQ-003: Districts require auditability for asset chain-of-custody and admin actions. Sources: [TSDS privacy guidance](https://www.texasstudentdatasystem.org/TSDS/About/Security/Privacy) (accountability expectations)
- TX-REQ-004: Districts require documented data retention and deletion practices. Sources: [TSDS privacy guidance](https://www.texasstudentdatasystem.org/TSDS/About/Security/Privacy), PEIMS reporting scope ([TEC § 48.009](https://texas.public.law/statutes/tex._educ._code_section_48.009))
- TX-REQ-005: Districts require incident reporting timelines and escalation paths. Sources: [TSDS privacy guidance](https://www.texasstudentdatasystem.org/TSDS/About/Security/Privacy) (security practices)

### Legal Sources (Initial)

- [Texas Government Code § 552.114](https://texas.public.law/statutes/tex._gov%27t_code_section_552.114) (confidentiality of student records). Summary: treats student records as confidential and limits disclosure.
- [Texas Education Code § 38.309](https://texas.public.law/statutes/tex._educ._code_section_38.309) (privacy of student information). Summary: requires school districts to adopt a privacy policy for student information and define safeguards.
- [Texas Education Code § 48.009](https://texas.public.law/statutes/tex._educ._code_section_48.009) (required PEIMS reporting; transferred from § 42.006). Summary: defines required student data reporting scope for state reporting.
- [TSDS privacy guidance](https://www.texasstudentdatasystem.org/TSDS/About/Security/Privacy) (references TEC § 42.006 and PEIMS scope). Summary: operational guidance for data handling, access, and privacy practices.
- [Texas Attorney General guidance](https://www.texasattorneygeneral.gov/es/node/4231) on redacting student records (references § 552.114). Summary: guidance on withholding or redacting student information from public records.
